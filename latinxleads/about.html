Our Mission & Learning Outcomes

Leadership, identity,
liderazgo.

Latinx Leads is built around four intentional learning outcome areas — designed to move every participant from awareness, to knowledge, to skills, to action.

Learning Outcomes · Area 1

Raise Awareness.

  • Recognize personal biases and unexamined assumptions
  • Understand intersectionality within the Latinx diaspora
  • Develop empathy across lines of difference
  • See themselves as agents of social change
Learning Outcomes · Area 2

Increase Knowledge.

  • Latin American history and the lived experience of our diasporas
  • Global diversity and cross-cultural understanding
  • A shared vocabulary for naming and discussing identity
  • The structural roots of inequity in our schools and communities
Learning Outcomes · Area 3

Develop Skills.

  • Inclusive leadership and facilitation
  • Asking open-ended questions that invite real dialogue
  • Standing up — respectfully and effectively
  • Building coalitions across identity lines
Learning Outcomes · Area 4

Plan for Social Action.

  • Service projects tied to real community needs
  • Cross-cultural interaction and sustained dialogue
  • Programming that shifts school & campus climate
  • Accountability structures to see the plan through
