The Conference

Identity, liderazgo,
comunidad.

Latinx Leads brings together student leaders from across the Latinx diaspora — our Mexican, Puerto Rican, Dominican, Caribbean, Central American, and South American students — to build the leadership, knowledge, and plans that move our communities forward.

Learning Outcomes →
/ 01

Raise Awareness

Identity, intersectionality, and the power of naming our experience.

/ 02

Increase Knowledge

Latin American history, global diversity, and a shared vocabulary.

/ 03

Develop Skills

Inclusive leadership, facilitation, and standing up with purpose.

/ 04

Plan Social Action

Concrete service projects tied to real community needs.
