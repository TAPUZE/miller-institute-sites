Who We Are

A training organization
with a mission.

The Ceceilyn Miller Institute (CMI) equips institutions and their people with the awareness, content knowledge, and skills to build cultures of inclusion and mutual respect. Our work spans K–12 schools, colleges and universities, corporate workplaces, and police and public-safety agencies.

In addition to on-site programs, CMI produces four national conferences — NSLDC, Black Students Lead, Latinx Leads, and the School Climate & Anti-Bullying Conference — bringing student leaders and educators together every year.
