Implicit Bias Training

Awareness, acceptance,
and the skill to monitor.

An experiential program that moves participants from theory to self-awareness to the practical skill of catching bias in the moment.

The Approach

From theory
to practice.

Participants experience a series of activities that raise their awareness of their own biases, promote multicultural awareness and acceptance, and teach the skills to monitor biases as they arise — at work, in the classroom, on the street, and at home.

What Participants Gain

Outcomes of the training.

/ 01

Raise Awareness of Biases

Experiential activities surface implicit biases that otherwise go unnamed.

/ 02

Promote Multicultural Acceptance

Moving beyond tolerance toward genuine multicultural appreciation.

/ 03

Learn to Monitor Bias

Practical skills to recognize bias in real time and redirect behavior.

/ 04

Apply Across Contexts

Transferable frameworks for the workplace, classroom, and community.
