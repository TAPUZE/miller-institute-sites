Colleges & Universities

Ten programs for
the campus community.

CMI's higher-education portfolio serves student leaders, advisors, faculty, and administrators across public and private campuses nationwide.

/ 01

Diversity & Inclusion 101

A foundational workshop introducing core concepts, vocabulary, and the case for inclusion.

/ 02

Creating Effective Diversity Programs

A how-to for student organizations and departments designing programs that move the needle.

/ 03

Race & Racism on College Campuses

Honest, evidence-based dialogue on how race shows up in campus life.

/ 04

Bridging the Gender Divide

Exploring gender equity, representation, and the culture shifts that drive change.

/ 05

Understanding & Empathy for LGBTQ Students

Building a campus culture where LGBTQ students are seen and celebrated.

/ 06

Understanding Privilege in All Forms

A facilitated exploration of privilege — and how to use it for good.

/ 07

Women Empowerment

Leadership, agency, and voice for women across campus life.

/ 08

Preventing Bullying, Cyberbullying & Hazing

Strategies for a campus climate without hazing or harassment.

/ 09

Residential Life Training

Custom training for RAs, peer mentors, and live-in staff.

/ 10

Greek Life & Student Org Training

Identity, power, and accountability for student-led organizations.
