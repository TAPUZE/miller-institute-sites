About NSLDC

Where student leaders
shape the future.

Through experiential workshops, keynote sessions, and round table discussions, teams of students explore various elements of diversity and how they affect themselves and their campuses.

During the conference, students are led through facilitated workshops to develop a Campus Action Plan — a program or event that the team selects to work on together in order to bring a piece of the NSLDC experience back to their own campuses.
