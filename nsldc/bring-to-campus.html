On-Campus Training

Bring NSLDC to
your campus.

NSLDC provides student leaders with training and facilitation sessions on Diversity and Inclusion — delivered directly to your campus. Our curriculum equips participants with the awareness, knowledge, and skills needed to foster mutual respect.

Request a Program
Signature Workshops

Eight curricula — ready for your campus.

Each workshop is led by NSLDC facilitators and tailored to your campus context, scale, and goals.

/ 01

Diversity & Inclusion 101

A foundational workshop introducing core concepts, vocabulary, and the case for inclusion.

/ 02

Creating Effective Diversity Programs

A how-to for student organizations and departments designing programs that actually move the needle.

/ 03

Race and Racism on College Campuses

Honest, evidence-based dialogue on how race shows up in campus life and what leaders can do.

/ 04

Bridging the Gender Divide

Exploring gender equity, representation, and the culture shifts that drive real change.

/ 05

Understanding & Empathy for LGBTQ Students

Building a campus culture in which LGBTQ students are seen, supported, and celebrated.

/ 06

Understanding Privilege in All Forms

A facilitated exploration of privilege beyond the obvious — and how to use it for good.

/ 07

Women Empowerment

Leadership, agency, and voice for women across every sector of campus life.

/ 08

Preventing Bullying, Cyberbullying & Hazing

Strategies for creating a campus climate where hazing and harassment have no home.
