Two Days · One Purpose
The Conference

Leadership, diversity,
social justice.

The Black Students Lead Conference is designed for Black high school and college student leaders — and for the advisors who support them. Together, we raise awareness, deepen knowledge, build skills, and plan concrete social action rooted in our communities.

Learning Outcomes →
/ 01

Raise Awareness

Biases, intersectionality, empathy — and seeing ourselves as agents of change.

/ 02

Increase Knowledge

Black American history, global diversity, and a shared vocabulary.

/ 03

Develop Skills

Inclusive leadership, asking the right questions, standing up with purpose.

/ 04

Plan Social Action

Service projects and sustained cross-cultural interaction.
