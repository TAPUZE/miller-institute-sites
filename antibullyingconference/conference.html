Terms & Cancellation.

Terms & Conditions

By submitting your registration you are agreeing to register for this conference and will be billed accordingly. If you fail to attend the conference you will be held responsible for any balance that is remaining. Please note the conference cancellation policy prior to submitting this form. Registration information will be billed and submitted to the information provided.

Cancellation Policy

All cancellations must be received in writing 15 days before the conference date. Full refunds will be given only for cancellations received 15 days before the beginning date of this conference. After this time there will be a refund less a $30 processing fee per person and any credit-card processing fees. No refunds of any kind will be made within seven days of the conference.
